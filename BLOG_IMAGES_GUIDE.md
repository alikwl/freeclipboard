## Image Specifications

### Featured Images (Blog Cards)
- **Dimensions**: 1200x630px
- **Format**: WebP
- **File size**: Under 200KB
- **Purpose**: Blog listing page thumbnails

### Hero Images (Top of Article)
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px or 1600x900px
- **Format**: WebP
- **File size**: Under 300KB
- **Purpose**: Main article header image

### Content Images (In-Article)
- **Dimensions**: 1200x800px or 1000x667px
- **Format**: WebP
- **File size**: Under 150KB
- **Purpose**: Supporting visuals within articles

## Image Creation Tips

### Tools You Can Use

**Free Tools:**
- Canva (canva.com) - Easy templates and design
- Figma (figma.com) - Professional design tool
- Unsplash (unsplash.com) - Free stock photos
- Pexels (pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- DALL-E or Midjourney - AI-generated images

**Paid Tools:**
- Adobe Photoshop
- Adobe Illustrator
- Shutterstock (stock photos)

### Design Guidelines

**Color Scheme:**
- Primary: #3B82F6 (blue)
- Secondary: #8B5CF6 (purple)
- Accent: #10B981 (green)
- Use gradients for modern look

**Style:**
- Modern and clean
- Professional but approachable
- Consistent across all images
- Use icons and illustrations
- Avoid cluttered designs

**Text on Images:**
- Use readable fonts (Open Sans, Montserrat)
- High contrast text
- Minimal text (let the article do the talking)
- Include your brand/logo subtly

### WebP Conversion

After creating images, convert them to WebP format:

1. Use our [Image Converter Tool](/tools/image-converter/)
2. Upload your PNG or JPG
3. Select WebP format
4. Choose quality: 80-85% for photos, 90%+ for graphics
5. Download and save to `assets/images/blog/`

## Image Optimization Checklist

Before adding images to your blog:

✅ Convert to WebP format
✅ Optimize file size (under recommended limits)
✅ Use descriptive file names
✅ Add proper alt text in blog posts
✅ Test loading speed
✅ Verify images display correctly on mobile
✅ Check image quality on different screens
