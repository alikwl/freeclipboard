Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Introduction

At FreeClipboard, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and tools.

Data Collection

FreeClipboard tools are designed so the text and files you process stay in your browser. We do not upload tool input to a FreeClipboard server for conversion or analysis.

What We Don't Collect Through the Tools Themselves:

  • The text you paste into a formatter, converter, or generator
  • Files you process in browser-based tools
  • Clipboard entries stored only in your own browser storage
  • Passwords or secrets generated locally on your device

How Our Tools Work

All our tools run entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • Processing happens locally on your computer
  • We cannot access your information

Local Storage

Some tools (like the Clipboard Manager) use your browser's localStorage to save data locally on your device. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Can be cleared by you at any time
  • Is not shared with third parties

Website Analytics and Advertising

Like many websites, FreeClipboard may use third-party services for site analytics and advertising. These services can process technical information such as IP address, browser details, approximate location, referring pages, and ad interactions.

These services help us understand site usage and fund the free tools, but they do not receive the actual text or files you work with inside the browser-based tools unless you intentionally submit that information somewhere else, such as by email.

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure performance, and limit fraud. Depending on your settings, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. You can learn more or adjust ad personalization through Google's Ads Settings.

Analytics

If you allow analytics cookies, we may use Google Analytics or similar measurement tools to understand aggregate site usage such as page views, device types, and traffic sources.

Cookies

We use a cookie consent banner so you can manage optional analytics and preference cookies. Advertising technologies may also be used when ads are served on the site.

Types of Cookies We Use:

1. Necessary Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

  • cookie_consent: Stores your cookie preferences (expires after 365 days)

2. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

  • _ga, _gid, _gat: Google Analytics cookies for tracking site usage

3. Marketing Cookies (Optional)

These technologies may be used by advertising partners such as Google when ads are shown on the site. They can support ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and, where permitted, personalized advertising.

  • Google AdSense cookies: Used to serve personalized advertisements

4. Preference Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enable the website to remember choices you make (such as your language or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

  • theme_preference: Stores your dark/light mode preference
  • language_preference: Stores your language selection

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit our site, you can choose which cookies to accept
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies
  • Opt-Out Links: Visit Google's Ads Settings to opt out of personalized advertising

Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on our website.

Your Rights

You control any tool data stored locally in your browser. For third-party cookies or analytics identifiers, you can use our cookie controls, your browser settings, or the privacy tools provided by the relevant third-party service.

Children's Privacy

Our services are available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
