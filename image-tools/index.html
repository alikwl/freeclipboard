❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our image tools

Are these tools really free?

Yes! All our image tools are 100% free with no hidden costs, registration requirements, or usage limits.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit your images.

What image formats do you support?

Our image converter supports PNG, JPG, and WebP formats with options for resizing and compression.

Can I customize QR codes?

Yes! You can customize QR code size, error correction level, and download as PNG or SVG format.

Do QR codes expire?

No. QR codes generated by our tool never expire and will work forever.

What color formats does the color picker support?

Our color picker supports HEX, RGB, and HSL formats with instant conversion between them.

Can I use these tools on mobile?

Absolutely! All tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

Is there a file size limit for image conversion?

Processing happens in your browser, so limits depend on your device. Most images up to 10MB work well.
