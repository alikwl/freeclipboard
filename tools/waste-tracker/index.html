💡 Zero Waste Tips

  • Start Small: Begin with one swap at a time. Don't overwhelm yourself!
  • Reusable Bags: Keep reusable bags in your car or by the door.
  • Buy in Bulk: Reduce packaging by buying bulk items with your own containers.
  • Refuse Single-Use: Say no to straws, plastic cutlery, and disposable cups.
  • Compost: Turn food scraps into nutrient-rich soil instead of trash.
  • Repair, Don't Replace: Fix broken items instead of buying new ones.
