About This Tool

The FreeClipboard Online Notepad is a fast, distraction-free writing space that lives entirely in your browser. No account creation, no app to install, no slow loading screens — just open the page and start typing. Your notes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage every 2 seconds, so you never lose a single word even if you accidentally close the tab.

Unlike basic text editors, this notepad supports multiple tabs so you can keep separate notes for different projects or topics. It also features dark mode for comfortable late-night writing, adjustable font sizes, one-click download as a .txt file, and detailed live stats including word count, character count, and line count. Everything runs locally in your browser — your notes never leave your device and are never visible to us or anyone else.

How to Use the Online Notepad

  1. Click anywhere in the text area and start typing — no setup or login required
  2. Your notes are saved automatically every 2 seconds; the status bar at the bottom confirms each save
  3. Click the + button next to the tabs to create a new note tab (up to 5 tabs)
  4. Double-click any tab name to rename it for easier organization
  5. Use the A- and A+ buttons to decrease or increase the font size
  6. Click 🌙 Dark to switch to dark mode — great for low-light environments
  7. Click 📋 Copy All to copy your entire note to your system clipboard in one click
  8. Click 💾 Download to save your note as a .txt file on your computer
  9. Press Ctrl+S at any time for an instant manual save with a confirmation toast
  10. Press Tab inside the editor to insert 4 spaces for indentation (great for code and lists)

Tips & Best Practices

  • Rename your tabs immediately after creating them — a tab named "Blog Draft" is far easier to find than "Note 3"
  • Use the Download button regularly if you write important content, since clearing browser data will erase localStorage
  • The Tab key shortcut makes this notepad surprisingly effective for writing code, structured lists, or outlined notes
  • Dark mode + large font size is the most comfortable setting for long writing sessions at night
  • Keep one tab as a permanent "Inbox" for random thoughts throughout the day, and create project-specific tabs only when needed
  • The Copy All button makes it easy to paste your note directly into email, Google Docs, or any other tool in one step
  • Because notes auto-save to localStorage, different browsers and devices have separate note storage — use Download + paste to move notes between devices

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the online notepad save automatically?

Yes — your notes are saved to your browser's local storage automatically every 2 seconds. You can also press Ctrl+S at any time for an instant manual save. The status bar at the bottom of the editor always shows the last save time.

Is my notepad data private?

Completely. All notes are stored only in your browser's local storage on your own device. Nothing is sent to our servers, no account is required, and no one else can access your notes. They are as private as files saved on your hard drive.

What happens if I close the browser tab?

Nothing bad — your notes are auto-saved every 2 seconds to local storage. When you reopen the page, all your tabs and their content will be restored exactly as you left them.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes. The notepad is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers on iPhone and Android. The toolbar adapts to smaller screens, and the editor is touch-friendly. For the best mobile experience, use it in your phone's browser just like on desktop.

How many notes/tabs can I create?

You can have up to 5 note tabs open at the same time. Each tab saves its content independently. If you need more, simply download completed notes as .txt files to free up tab slots.

Will my notes be deleted if I clear browser history?

Yes — clearing your browser's local storage or site data will delete your saved notes. We strongly recommend downloading important notes as .txt files using the Download button before clearing browser data.

Does this work without internet?

After your first visit, the page and all its features work completely offline. Auto-save, dark mode, tabs, and downloads all function without any internet connection.

How is this different from the Clipboard Manager on this site?

The Clipboard Manager is designed for storing and organizing many short copied items (snippets, URLs, code) with search, tags, and categories. The Online Notepad is a continuous writing space for longer, ongoing notes — more like a traditional text editor. Use both together for a complete workflow.
