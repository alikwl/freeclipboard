Does the handwriting look real?

Absolutely. We use a "humanize" algorithm that adds slight imperfections, variable spacing, and tilt to characters, making it indistinguishable from real pen-on-paper writing. The realistic variations ensure your handwritten text doesn't look computer-generated.

Can I download the output as a PDF?

Yes, you can export your handwritten notes as high-quality images (PNG) or compile them into a PDF document for easy submission, printing, or sharing.

Is my data safe?

Your privacy is our priority. This tool runs locally on your browser using JavaScript. No text or data is ever uploaded to our servers. Everything happens on your device, ensuring complete confidentiality.
