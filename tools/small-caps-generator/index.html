Small Caps Generator – Convert Text to Unicode Small Capitals

Free Small Caps Generator. Convert text to Unicode small capital letters to make tweets, bios, and usernames look like Tʜɪs. Copy in one click.

Convert normal text into Unicode small capital letters — perfect for tweets, bios, and headings.

Automatically updates the preview when you type or paste.
If a small-cap variant doesn’t exist (e.g., “s”, “x”), keep the original character.

Benefits

  • Stand out on social media with eye-catching small caps.
  • Works anywhere that supports Unicode text.
  • Copy in one click; fully client-side and private.
  • Mobile-friendly and fast — no sign-up required.

How to Use

  1. Type or paste your text in the input area.
  2. Enable Convert as you type for instant updates, or click Convert.
  3. Click Copy Output to copy the small caps text.
  4. Paste into tweets, bios, usernames, or anywhere Unicode is supported.

Notes

Not all letters have official “small capital” Unicode versions. Characters like s and x may remain unchanged depending on your settings. We preserve unsupported characters by default to keep your text readable and safe.

Best Practices for Bios & Tweets

  • Keep it short and readable; use small caps for emphasis, not every word.
  • Combine with emojis sparingly to avoid visual clutter.
  • Use Title Case before conversion for cleaner headings; use Lowercase for ALL-CAPS inputs.
  • Preview on your platform to ensure Unicode renders correctly.
