    ℹ️ Understanding Your Results

    kWh (Kilowatt-hour)

    The unit of energy used on your electricity bill. 1 kWh = 1000 watts used for 1 hour.

    Carbon Intensity

    How much CO₂ is produced per kWh. Varies by region based on energy sources (coal, gas, renewable).

    Energy Star

    Look for Energy Star certified appliances - they use 10-50% less energy than standard models.
