Image Converter FAQ

Does this tool upload my images?

No. All conversions happen in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

Which formats are supported?

PNG, JPG, WebP, and AVIF (if supported by your browser). SVG input is supported and can be converted to raster formats.

How do I keep transparency?

Use PNG or WebP. For JPG, enable "Flatten transparency" and choose a background color.

Can I convert multiple images?

Yes. Upload multiple files, use Convert All, and download each or all at once.
