🏷️ Meta Tag Generator

Beginners‑friendly visual form. Generate clean <meta> tags for SEO, social sharing, and favicons.

Recommended 1200×630, JPEG/PNG.

Quick Tips

  • Keep descriptions concise (≤ 160 chars).
  • Use absolute URLs for images and canonical links.
  • Match Twitter and Open Graph titles and descriptions.
  • Pick a contrasting theme-color for mobile browsers.

Benefits

  • One form for SEO + social cards.
  • Valid HTML with required attributes.
  • Copy/paste into your <head> instantly.
  • Free and fast generator with clean output.

FAQ

Where do I paste these tags?

Paste into your page <head> section. Most frameworks expose a way to set head metadata.

Do I need both Open Graph and Twitter?

Yes, for consistent previews across platforms. They can share the same values.

What size should the OG image be?

Use 1200×630 (landscape) for best quality.
