Before You Email

A little context in your message helps me reply faster.

How should I report a broken tool?

Include the tool URL, what you clicked, the result you expected, and what actually happened. Browser and device details help a lot.

Can I suggest a new tool?

Yes. The best suggestions explain a real problem, who it affects, and why the existing tools on the site do not already solve it.

Do you offer paid accounts or custom plans?

No paid subscription plans are offered right now. FreeClipboard is a free, ad-supported tools site.

Do you read every email?

Yes, although reply times can vary because the site is run by one person.

Need Help With a Tool?

Browse the site, read the tool instructions, or send a direct email if you are stuck.
