Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond within 24 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.

Can you add feature X to tool Y?

We love hearing feature requests! Please be as specific as possible about what you'd like to see and how it would help you.

I found a bug, what should I include?

Please include: which tool, what you were trying to do, what happened instead, your browser and OS. Screenshots help a lot!

Do you offer custom development?

We focus on our free tools, but we're open to partnerships and collaborations. Contact us to discuss your needs.
