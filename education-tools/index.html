What is spaced repetition?

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that schedules reviews at optimal intervals to maximize memory retention. Our flashcard tool uses the proven SM-2 algorithm.

It analyzes your headlines for SEO factors including length, power words, sentiment, readability, and type to give you a comprehensive score and actionable feedback.

Is my study data private?

Yes! All your flashcard decks and study progress are stored locally in your browser. No data is sent to our servers.

Can I export my flashcards?

Yes! You can export and import flashcard decks as JSON files for backup or sharing.

How many flashcards can I create?

Unlimited! Create as many decks and cards as you need. There are no restrictions or limits.

Do the tools work offline?

Yes! Once loaded, both tools work completely offline. Perfect for studying anywhere.
