Everything you need to know about our clipboard tools

Are these tools really free?

Yes! All our clipboard and text tools are 100% free with no hidden costs, registration requirements, or usage limits.

Is my clipboard data stored on your servers?

No. All data is stored locally in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit your clipboard items or text.

Can I export my clipboard history?

Yes! The clipboard manager includes export functionality to save your items as JSON or text files.

How accurate is the word counter?

Very accurate. Our word counter uses industry-standard algorithms to count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs.

Does the clipboard manager work offline?

Yes! Once loaded, all tools work completely offline. Your data is stored locally in your browser.

What's the difference between word counter and character counter?

Word counter focuses on words, sentences, and reading time. Character counter provides detailed character-level analysis including letters, numbers, and special characters.

Can I use these tools on mobile?

Absolutely! All tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

How many items can I store in clipboard manager?

There's no hard limit. Storage depends on your browser's local storage capacity, which is typically several megabytes.
