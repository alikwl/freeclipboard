❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our clipboard tools

What is a clipboard manager?

A clipboard manager stores your copy history, allowing you to access previously copied items. Our free clipboard manager saves unlimited items with search, categories, and favorites.

How accurate is the word counter?

Our word counter is highly accurate and uses the same counting method as Microsoft Word and Google Docs. It counts words separated by spaces and provides real-time statistics.

Can I use these tools offline?

Yes! After the first visit, all clipboard and text tools work offline. Your data is processed locally in your browser with no internet connection needed.

Is my clipboard data private?

Absolutely! All data stays in your browser. We never upload, store, or access your clipboard content. Your privacy is our top priority.

What's the difference between word counter and character counter?

The word counter focuses on counting words, sentences, paragraphs, and reading time. The character counter breaks down text into letters, numbers, spaces, and special characters.

Can I export my clipboard history?

Yes! Our clipboard manager supports both export and import functionality. Save your clipboard history as a file and restore it anytime on any device.
