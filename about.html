Everything you need to know about FreeClipboard

Who created FreeClipboard?

FreeClipboard was created by a team of developers passionate about providing free, privacy-focused tools for everyone. We believe essential productivity tools should be accessible without compromising security.

Why are all tools free?

We believe essential productivity tools should be accessible to everyone. We're ad-supported to keep everything free while maintaining quality and privacy.

How do you protect user privacy?

All our tools run entirely in your browser. Your data never touches our servers. We don't track, collect, or store any user information.

Can I contribute or suggest new tools?

Yes! We welcome suggestions and feedback. Contact us with your ideas and we'll consider adding them to our collection.
