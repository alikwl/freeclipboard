How to Automate Your Workflow

1

Download & Install

Download the extension ZIP file, extract it, and load it into Chrome via chrome://extensions. Enable "Developer mode" and click "Load unpacked" to select the extracted folder.

2

Open Flow Veo

Navigate to the Flow Veo 3 platform in your Chrome browser. Make sure you're logged into your account and on the video generation page.

3

Upload Prompts & Start

Open the extension popup and upload a TXT file containing your video prompts (one prompt per line). You can use your own prompts file or download our sample to test the extension.

📄 Download Sample Prompts

How it works: After uploading your prompts file and clicking "Start", the extension will automatically input the first prompt, wait 4 minutes for video generation, auto-download the video, then move to the next prompt. This loop continues until all prompts are processed.

4

Relax

Let the extension do the work! It will automatically input prompts, wait for videos to generate, download them, and repeat the process. You can leave it running and come back to a folder full of videos.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work with the free version of Flow Veo?

Yes, it works with any account type.

Why the 4-minute delay?

This ensures the previous video renders completely and protects your account from spam detection.

Where do the videos go?

They will be saved to your browser's default 'Downloads' folder.
