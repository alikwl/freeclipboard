What hash algorithms do you support?

Our hash generator supports MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, SHA-384, and SHA-512 algorithms.

Yes! We generate UUID v4 using cryptographically secure random numbers, ensuring uniqueness with extremely high probability.

Yes! Once loaded, all tools work completely offline. Your data is generated locally in your browser.

Yes! Our password generator supports bulk generation, allowing you to create multiple passwords simultaneously.
