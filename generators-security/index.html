❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our security tools

Are the generated passwords secure?

Yes! We use cryptographically secure random number generation (crypto.getRandomValues) to ensure maximum security.

Do you store generated passwords?

No. All generation happens locally in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit any generated data.

What hash algorithms do you support?

Our hash generator supports MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, SHA-384, and SHA-512 algorithms.

Are UUIDs truly unique?

Yes! We generate UUID v4 using cryptographically secure random numbers, ensuring uniqueness with extremely high probability.

Can I use these tools offline?

Yes! Once loaded, all tools work completely offline. Your data is generated locally in your browser.

How strong should my password be?

We recommend at least 16 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols for maximum security.

Can I generate multiple passwords at once?

Yes! Our password generator supports bulk generation, allowing you to create multiple passwords simultaneously.

Are these tools free for commercial use?

Absolutely! All tools are free to use for both personal and commercial projects without any restrictions.
